Bio

Hi. My name is Karlis, but my friends call me Sheen. I've been associated with music for more than 15 years, playing in several bands and projects including Bloody Heels and Erased By Sunrise. At the start of 2020 when the COVID-19 pandemic hit the world, I took the time to the explore the world of music production, which led to this project - Karlis Sheen. A mixture of rock, pop, rap and electronic music encompassing themes of daily struggles, relationships, mental health and whatever else has been on my mind lately. So if you are into groovy basslines, hard hitting drums, electronic elements and "stanky" guitar riffs, check out my music!

Music

"True Friends"

Released: 28.07.2022.

“True Friends” is a little under three minute long lookback at ended friendships, self-respect and most importantly - standing up for yourself. “I assume I'm not the only one who has had to experience disappointment and disrespect from friends. Feeling that you are not being heard, not being treated as an equal, or being badly let down. This song is like an answer to those situations when it was not given.”

"Stay The F*** Away From Me"

Released: 05.05.2022.

“Stay The F*** Away From Me” is a song about being involved in a toxic relationship, where one of party's can't seem to make up their mind on what they actually want out of this relationship. This then leads to, well, let's just say - “frequent physical interactions” leaving the other person feeling used and hurt. This song serves a message about these types of relationships, considering how common they are nowadays as well as to encourage people to end them.

"Life As It Is"

Released: 01.04.2022.

“Life As It Is” is a song about reflecting on some of the shortcomings you as a person can have and how you should not be ashamed of them. It talks about coming to an understanding that life will not always be in your favor. Its message is to inspire people to be true to themselves and to proceed through life on their own terms.
